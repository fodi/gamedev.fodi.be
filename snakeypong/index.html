SnakeyPong

About a week ago I've unearthed an old doodle (a simple "free angle" snake) and made the food bounce around.

You control a snake with the keyboard (press A / D) or the touch screen (tap the left / right side) and you have to pick up the food which bounces off the walls and the snake as well. I have a bunch of ideas to try out, but it's already fairly fun, although it's a bit of a performance hog at the moment.

Check out where this project goes (usually nowhere, but who knows), try SnakeyPong on Itch!
