Dungeon Bouncer

Dungeon Bouncer is the working title of a fairly simple idea from this list: essentially a dungeon crawler / physics puzzle, where you move the player like in 2D top down golf games.

Update 1: the first build is up on Itch. It has basic movement (touch/mouse drag aim, limited number of bounces), walls and pickups.

Update 2: added the first moving enemies.
