About me

I'm Fodi, a millennial web & game developer from Hungary. I make games in Construct 3, sometimes live on Twitch (VODs are on YouTube). In 2025 I started a weekly meetup called Gamedev Club in my hometown. I'm half of Lasermagnet Games.
