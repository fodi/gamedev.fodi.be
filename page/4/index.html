About me

I'm Fodi, a millennial web & game developer from Hungary, 50% of Lasermagnet Games. I make games in Construct 3, sometimes live on Twitch (VODs are on YouTube). I have a separate site called Advanced Construct, where I write about Construct 3 development.

In 2025 I started a weekly meetup called Gamedev Club in my hometown to connect with fellow hobbyist devs.
